Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. Here is everything you need to know about deleting your Antempo account and data.

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This action is irreversible

Once your account deletion is finalized, all your data is permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered. This includes your focus history, tasks, club memberships, badges, streaks, and subscription status. Please make sure you truly want to proceed before confirming deletion.

How to delete your account

Follow these steps to delete your account directly from the Antempo app.

1

Open Antempo and go to the "You" tab

Launch the app and tap the "You" icon in the bottom navigation bar. This is your profile and settings area.

2

Open Settings

Scroll down and tap Settings (the gear icon). This opens the full settings panel where you manage your account preferences.

3

Tap "Delete Account & Data"

Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen. Under the Account section, tap "Delete Account & Data". This option is displayed in red for visibility.

4

Confirm deletion

A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to verify your decision. Tap "Delete My Account" to proceed. You will be signed out immediately and the 30-day grace period begins.

What data gets deleted

When your account deletion is finalized, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

👤
Account Information
Your email address, display name, profile photo, and authentication credentials.
⏱
Focus Sessions
All recorded focus sessions, durations, timestamps, and completion history.
📋
Tasks & Plans
All tasks, weekly plans, priority tags, categories, and review notes you created.
📊
Analytics & Progress
Statistics, streaks, XP, badges, rank progress, and heatmap data.
👫
Club Memberships
Your membership in all study clubs, including leaderboard contributions and chat messages.
⚙
Preferences
Timer settings, notification preferences, soundscape configurations, and app customizations.

What happens to your clubs

When you delete your account, the following happens to your club memberships:

  • Clubs you are a member of: You are automatically removed from the club. Your historical leaderboard contributions are anonymized (displayed as "Deleted User").
  • Clubs you own: Ownership is transferred to the next most active member. If no other members exist, the club is permanently deleted.
  • Co-focus sessions: Any active co-focus sessions you are part of will end for you immediately. Other participants are not affected.

Data retention period

After you confirm deletion, your account enters a 30-day grace period. During this time:

Changed your mind? Simply sign back in with your original credentials within 30 days to cancel the deletion and restore your account with all data intact.

  • Your account is immediately deactivated and inaccessible to other users.
  • Your data is retained on our servers for 30 days in case you change your mind.
  • After 30 days, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our servers and backups.
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (not tied to your identity) may be retained for product improvement.
Day 0
Deletion confirmed
Days 1-30
Grace period (recoverable)
Day 31+
Permanently deleted

Important: Cancel your subscription first

Deleting your Antempo account does not automatically cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription. To avoid continued charges:

  • iOS: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Antempo > Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Open Google Play > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > Antempo > Cancel
✉
Prefer to request deletion by email?

If you cannot access the app or prefer not to use the in-app option, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please send your request from the email address associated with your Antempo account.

antempostudios@gmail.com

Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 48 hours and confirm via email.
