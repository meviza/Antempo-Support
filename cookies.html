Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Antempo Studios ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use the Antempo application. By continuing to use our services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website or use a web-based application. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you interact with content, and improve your overall experience.

Cookies may be "session" cookies, which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent" cookies, which remain on your device for a set period or until you manually delete them.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our website and services. They enable core functionality such as authentication, security, and session management. Without these cookies, our services cannot function properly.

  • Authentication tokens - Keep you signed in across sessions and verify your identity
  • Session identifiers - Maintain your active session state while using the app
  • Security cookies - Protect against cross-site request forgery and other threats
  • Load balancing - Distribute traffic across our servers for optimal performance

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website and application. They collect information about page views, navigation paths, and feature usage in an aggregated and anonymized form. This data helps us identify areas for improvement and measure the effectiveness of new features.

  • Page view tracking - Which pages and screens are visited most frequently
  • Session duration - How long users spend on different parts of the app
  • Feature engagement - Which features are used and how often
  • Error reporting - Crash data and error logs to help us fix issues quickly

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies remember your settings and choices so you do not have to re-enter them each time you visit. They make your experience more convenient and personalized.

  • Language preference - Your selected language (Antempo supports 13 languages)
  • Theme settings - Your preferred display mode (light, dark, or system)
  • Notification preferences - Your chosen notification and reminder settings
  • Onboarding state - Whether you have completed the initial setup flow

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services that may set their own cookies on your device. We carefully select these partners and only work with providers that maintain strong privacy and security standards.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage statistics about our website and marketing pages. Google Analytics uses cookies to identify unique visitors, track session information, and analyse traffic patterns. The data collected is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

  • _ga - Distinguishes unique users. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_* - Maintains session state. Expires after 2 years.
  • _gid - Distinguishes unique users. Expires after 24 hours.
  • _gat - Throttles request rate. Expires after 1 minute.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Firebase

Antempo uses Firebase, a Google-operated platform, for authentication, real-time data synchronization, crash reporting, and performance monitoring. Firebase may use cookies and similar technologies to provide these services.

  • Firebase Authentication - Manages user sign-in sessions and token refresh
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Collects anonymized crash reports to improve app stability
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring - Tracks app startup time, network latency, and screen rendering

For more information, see Firebase Privacy and Security.

We do not use cookies for advertising. We do not sell cookie data to third parties. We do not allow third-party advertising networks to place cookies on our services.

4. How to Manage and Disable Cookies

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. You can manage or disable cookies using the methods below. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of Antempo.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these options under "Privacy", "Security", or "Cookies" in your browser preferences. Common actions include:

  • Viewing and deleting existing cookies
  • Blocking all cookies or only third-party cookies
  • Receiving a notification before a cookie is set
  • Setting cookies to expire when you close the browser

For browser-specific instructions:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

Mobile Devices

On iOS and Android, you can manage cookie behaviour through your device settings or within individual browser apps. Additionally, both platforms offer options to limit ad tracking and reset advertising identifiers, although Antempo does not use these for advertising purposes.

Google Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we present a cookie consent banner that explains our use of cookies and gives you the option to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your consent preference is stored so you are not asked again on subsequent visits.

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our website, or by contacting us directly.

Essential cookies do not require consent as they are necessary for the basic operation of the service. Analytics and preference cookies are only activated after you provide consent.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the app or via email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
