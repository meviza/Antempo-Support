FAQ

Common questions

Is Antempo really free?

Yes. The free tier includes the Pomodoro timer, basic task management, daily stats, and one study club. You can use Antempo indefinitely without paying.

What platforms are supported?

Antempo is available on iOS, Android, macOS, and web. Your data syncs across all devices through your account.

Can I use Antempo offline?

Absolutely. All core features including timers, tasks, and local stats work without an internet connection. Data syncs when you reconnect.

How do study clubs work?

Create a club and invite friends with a code. Start co-focus sessions together, compete on weekly leaderboards, and track collective progress.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can cancel through the App Store or Google Play at any time. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app, or visit our account deletion page for step-by-step instructions.
