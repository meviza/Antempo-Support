Getting Started

How do I create an Antempo account?

Download Antempo from the App Store or Google Play. When you first open the app, you can sign up using your email address or tap "Continue with Google" for one-tap sign-in. Your account lets you sync data across all your devices.

What is the Plan-Focus-Review-Grow cycle?

Antempo is built around a four-step productivity cycle. First, Plan your tasks and set priorities. Then Focus using the built-in timer. After each session, Review your progress with analytics. Finally, Grow by joining study clubs and tracking long-term streaks. This cycle is designed to build sustainable study habits.

Is Antempo free to use?

Yes. The free tier includes the Pomodoro timer, basic task management, daily statistics, one study club, and three ambient sounds. You can use Antempo indefinitely without paying. Premium ($3.99/month or $29.99/year) unlocks all timer modes, full analytics, unlimited clubs, all soundscapes, badges, and priority support.

What platforms does Antempo support?

Antempo is available on iOS, Android, macOS, and the web. Your data syncs automatically across all devices through your account. Core features like timers and tasks also work fully offline.

Account & Billing

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Open Antempo and go to the "You" tab. Tap "Upgrade to Premium" and choose between the monthly ($3.99/month) or yearly ($29.99/year) plan. Both include a 7-day free trial. Payment is processed through the App Store or Google Play.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store, not within Antempo itself. On iOS, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Antempo > Cancel. On Android, open Google Play > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > Antempo > Cancel. You will keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google according to their respective refund policies. On iOS, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. On Android, open the Google Play app and go to your order history. If you have trouble, contact us and we will do our best to help.

How do I change my email or password?

Go to the "You" tab and tap Settings. Under Account, you can update your email address or reset your password. If you signed in with Google, your email is managed through your Google account.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from the app. Go to the "You" tab > Settings > "Delete Account & Data". For detailed step-by-step instructions, visit our Account Deletion page.

Timer & Focus

What timer modes are available?

Antempo offers several timer modes. Pomodoro (25 min focus / 5 min break) is available on the free tier. Premium unlocks Deep Focus (52/17), Custom intervals (set your own work and break durations), and Infinite mode (focus until you decide to stop). Each mode tracks your time and awards XP.

Does the timer work offline?

Yes. The timer runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection. Your session data will be saved locally and synced to your account once you reconnect.

What are ambient soundscapes?

Ambient soundscapes are background audio tracks designed to help you concentrate. The free tier includes rain, library, and cafe. Premium unlocks additional environments like fireplace, ocean waves, forest, thunderstorm, and more. You can layer multiple sounds and adjust their individual volumes.

How do streaks and XP work?

You earn XP for every completed focus session. The amount depends on the duration and whether you completed the full interval without interruption. Consecutive daily sessions build your streak. Maintain your streak to climb the ranks from Beginner through Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Grandmaster.

Clubs & Social

How do I create or join a study club?

To create a club, go to the Clubs tab and tap "Create Club". Give it a name and share the invite code with friends. To join an existing club, tap "Join Club" and enter the code you received. Free users can join one club; Premium users can join unlimited clubs.

What are co-focus sessions?

Co-focus sessions let club members study together in real time. When a club member starts a session, others can see it and join. You will see who is currently focusing, creating a sense of shared accountability even when you are studying remotely.

How do club leaderboards work?

Club leaderboards rank members by total focus time each week. The leaderboard resets every Monday at midnight (your local time). Weekly challenges give bonus XP to the top three members. Leaderboards help make studying more engaging through friendly competition.

Can I leave a club?

Yes. Open the club, tap the settings icon, and select "Leave Club". Your personal focus data stays on your account. If you are the club owner, ownership transfers to the next most active member automatically.

Technical Issues

The app is not syncing across my devices

Make sure you are signed into the same account on all devices and have an active internet connection. Try pulling down on the main screen to force a sync. If the issue persists, sign out and sign back in. If problems continue, contact us with your account email and we will investigate.

The timer notification is not showing

Antempo needs notification permission to alert you when sessions end. On iOS, go to Settings > Notifications > Antempo and enable alerts. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > Antempo > Notifications. Also ensure the app is not restricted by battery optimization settings, which can prevent background notifications.

The app crashes on launch

First, ensure you have the latest version of Antempo installed. Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem continues, restart your device. As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app; your data is safely stored on our servers and will sync back when you sign in again.

How do I report a bug?

We appreciate bug reports. Send us an email at antempostudios@gmail.com with: (1) a description of what happened, (2) the steps to reproduce it, (3) your device model and OS version, and (4) a screenshot if possible. We will investigate and get back to you promptly.
